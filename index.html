Instruction & Rules

1. Charge is for increasing your power. With highter power you can cause highter damage. But be carefull, cube can roll numbers from 1 to 7. If you roll number 1, charging will fail and cause full damage to you! On the other side, if you roll number 7, you will use heal for 7 HP!

2. Attack is for dealing damage to your opponent. Your attack is equal your charged power!

3. If your HP will drop on 0, you are dead and your opponent is winner!

4. New game button is for reseting actual game.
